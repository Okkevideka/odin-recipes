Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are extremely easy to make! This will include:
  • 2 to 3 eggs
  • Butter or Olive oil
  • Salt and Black pepper

Steps

  1. Put butter on the pan and turn on the heat for the pan
  2. Then once it melts you would want to add the eggs into the pan
  3. After the eggs start to develop you should use a non metal item and mix the eggs up
  4. Lower the heat as you start to mix
  5. Add the salt and black pepper to the eggs
  6. You are finished!!!!
