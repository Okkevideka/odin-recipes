Cake!

Cake is always a very easy recipe to make! This will include:
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Last but not least least All purpose / plain flour

Steps

  1. First you would want to whip the eggs and sugar
  2. Start Gradually Add flour
  3. Use melted butter in the milk
  4. Mix some batter into the hot milk
  5. Slowly pour the mix mixture back into the whipped eggs
  6. Pour into 2 cake pans
  7. Bake for 30 minutes
  8. Cool upside down
  9. Decorate! Congrats you made a cake!

Making a cake is really easy and i'm glad you read, have a great rest of your day!
